Our History

Kizuna Alliance was founded in 2023 to help disadvantage communities to meet their needs in the region. Over the years the organisation has expanded it reach and impact, introducing programs that focus on education, empowerment, food and security

Our Mission

Misson is to help communities with basic need and support.

Our Vision

The vision of organisation is to create a world were every person has a chance to succeed and contribute positive to society.

Our Goals

  • Expand education support programs to reach more learners in underserved areas
  • Strengthen food security initiatives to reduce hunger in vulnerable communities
  • Promote sustainable community projects such as gardens and clean up drives
  • Empower youth through mentorship, skills training, and leadership opportunities
  • Build long term partnerships with sponsors, volunteers and local institutions
