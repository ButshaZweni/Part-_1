Organisation Overview
• Name
Kizuna Alliance 

• Brief History
Helpinfounded in 2023 to help disadvantage communities to meet their needs in the region. Over the years the organisation has expanded it reach and impact, introducing programs that focus on education, empowerment, food and security.

• Mission and Vision Statement 
Misson is to help communities with basic need and support. The vision of organisation is to create a world were every person has a chance to succeed and contribute positive to society.

The Target Audience includes local communities who needs help, Volunteers who wants to join in and potential donors who wants to change the world for the better.
